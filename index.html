About Me

I am a robotics researcher focused on deep learning, generative models, and reinforcement learning. My work often involves humanoid robots, manipulation, and navigation within simulated environments like Habitat.

Currently, I am exploring EEG-based Brain-Computer Interfaces (BCI) and developing SCALE-Net, a CNN-LSTM architecture for neural data processing.
